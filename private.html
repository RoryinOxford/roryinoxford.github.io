Private Oxford University & City Tour

Private Harry Potter Oxford Tour

Step inside the real-life Hogwarts and experience the magic of Harry Potter for yourself, on a tour of Oxford like no other. You’ll visit the locations where the movies were filmed, and see the incredible places that inspired the books in the first place. We’ll walk the corridors of Hogwarts in the New College cloisters, take our places at the Yule Ball in the Divinity School, and steal a peak of the Hogwarts Library at the Bodleian.

At the same time, you’ll discover what it’s like to study at the real-life Hogwarts, Oxford University, and see the most beautiful buildings the city has to offer. This tour includes entry into New College and the Divinity School – giving you a rare chance to go inside University buildings, and find out how they work for yourself.

As well as being perfect for all Harry Potter lovers, this tour is an excellent option for families who want to visit the University of Oxford in a way that’s just as exciting for their little witches and wizards.

Private C. S. Lewis & Tolkien Oxford Tour

Be transported through the real passageway into Narnia, have a drink in Lewis and Tolkien’s favourite spot, and explore the colleges that they called home: a C. S. Lewis and Tolkien Tour is the perfect way for all true inklings to visit Oxford. Alongside visiting the inspirations behind your favourite works, you’ll see the places where they were created. See the table on which Lord of the Rings was written, explore the colleges in which the Inklings came to be, and visit the building Lewis considered the most beautiful in England. At the same time, you’ll learn all about the City and University that cultivated these authors, led by an expert guide and graduate of the University of Oxford.

This tour is particularly customisable – options include expanding the tour to include Oxford literature more widely (e.g., Alice in Wonderland; Harry Potter; Philip Pullman and His Dark Materials; Iris Murdoch; Oscar Wilde; and G. M. Hopkins) or combining it with a tour of Oxford’s Christian History. Please enquire for more details.

Private Christian History Oxford Tour

Walk in the footsteps of the greats on this incredible tour of Oxford’s Christian heritage. As a recent theology graduate from the University of Oxford (BA Philosophy and Theology, 2020-2023), Rory is the perfect guide to the thousand-year story of Christianity in Oxford

On this tour, you’ll visit the sites of some of Western Christianity’s most turbulent moments, explore the incredible places in which Theology has been taught over the last thousand years, and see some of the most breathtaking church architecture in England. You’ll also find out what it’s like to study Theology today at the University of Oxford, and discover the fascinating history behind the University’s development.

Topics include: Reform and Reformation; the Wesley Brothers; Newman, Catholicism, and the Oxford Movement; and Tolkien, C. S. Lewis, and the Inklings. Tours can be made to focus on one, several, or all of these topics. Please enquire for sample itineraries for any of these options.
